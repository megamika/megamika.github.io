Snappy movement

Shape shifting

The standout feature of the game would be the ability to shift your form by destroying little terminals in the level.

Shifting your form would do several things
- Allow you to attack your enemies
- Make you move faster
- Add an ability to wall jump

The movement in the game was intentionally made to be very snappy and simple to fit with the pixel art stylistic of the game and the animation style.

Enemy AI

Although there is only one enemy in the game, the AI for that enemy is made to be modular and extendable to several types.

The game has the main "brain" script along with a visual scripting solution to manipulate their final behaviour and what the enemy would actually do.

Cutscene maker

The game had a story which was delivered through cutscenes. In our team we had a person who was responsible for making those cutscenes and the story for them.

Making a visually readable system where the artist/writer could make cutscenes in editor without having to touch code was a convinient way to speed up the process without needing to go back and forth.
